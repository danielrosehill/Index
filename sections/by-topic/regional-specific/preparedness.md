## Israel Preparedness Index [![View Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/view-repo-green)](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Israel-Preparedness-Index)
Index of repositories related to civilian emergency preparedness in Israel

- [HFC-Guidance-Flip-Book](https://github.com/danielrosehill/HFC-Guidance-Flip-Book) - Pikud HaOref guidance in a visual presentation for younger audiences

- [Israel-Civilian-Readiness-Guide](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Israel-Civilian-Readiness-Guide) - A consolidated series of readiness checklists, SOPs, and peacetime prep guides. Intended for civilian use in Israel. Non-official, but based on gov resources.

- [Pikud-Haoref-Guidelines-0326](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Pikud-Haoref-Guidelines-0326) - Extracted guidelines for use as a ground truth for AI agents

- [SITREP-Examples](https://github.com/danielrosehill/SITREP-Examples) - Examples of situational reports (SITREPs) to guide agentic generation or general authorship

- [Red-Alert-Geodash](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Red-Alert-Geodash) - Real-time dashboard for Israel's Pikud HaOref rocket/situation alerts with multi-map Leaflet views and polygon overlays

- [Red-Alert-Monitoring-Stack](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Red-Alert-Monitoring-Stack) - Microservices Docker stack for Pikud HaOref red alert monitoring, home automation, and real-time visualization

- [Oref-Alert-Proxy](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Oref-Alert-Proxy) - Local relay proxy for Israel Homefront Command (Pikud HaOref) alert data

- [Red-Alert-Pushover](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Red-Alert-Pushover) - Pushover notifications for large-scale Pikud HaOref alert events

- [Red-Alert-Actuator](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Red-Alert-Actuator) - Physical alert outputs (Snapcast TTS + MQTT lights) for Pikud HaOref alerts

- [Red-Alert-Telegram-Bot](https://github.com/danielrosehill/Red-Alert-Telegram-Bot) - On-demand situational intelligence Telegram bot for Pikud HaOref alerts
